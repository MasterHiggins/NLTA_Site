Details from the NLTA AGM events and meeting schedule, including the 2016 38th and 40th annual highlights.

2016 AGM Highlights

This AGM was held at the Community Centre in Grand Codroy, Codroy Valley on August 20 and 21, 2016.

Schedule of Events

  • Friday, August 19 — 8:00pm Director meeting to finalize the AGM agenda
  • Saturday, August 20 — 9:00am Registration
  • Saturday, August 20 — 10:00am AGM and guest speakers
  • Saturday, August 20 — 12:00pm Lunch
  • Saturday, August 20 — 1:00pm AGM continues and election of directors
  • Saturday, August 20 — 3:30pm Auction
  • Saturday, August 20 — 5:30pm Complimentary BBQ and children’s games
  • Saturday, August 20 — 8:00pm Directors meeting and executive election
  • Saturday, August 20 — 9:00pm Entertainment by local musicians
  • Sunday, August 21 — 9:00am to 12:00pm Adult trap setting and log sawing
