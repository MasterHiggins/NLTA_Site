Membership

Support the future of trapping in Newfoundland & Labrador

Join the NLTA to protect trapping rights, stay connected with the community, and receive the Canadian Trapper magazine as part of your annual membership.

Become an NLTA Member

Our association represents trappers in consultations with the Wildlife Division and supports responsible wildlife management through community action.

Affordable Membership

Annual membership is only $30 and includes a subscription to THE CANADIAN TRAPPER magazine, published five times each year.

Your Voice at Government Tables

NLTA advocates for trapping rights and gives members a collective voice with agencies and decision-makers.

Community Connection

Stay informed about trapping news, conservation issues, events, and training opportunities across the province.

Why your support matters

Membership helps keep trapping affordable, protects our heritage, and ensures that trappers are represented at every stage of resource management and policy development.

By joining NLTA, you are supporting yourself, your family, and the future of trapping in Newfoundland & Labrador.
